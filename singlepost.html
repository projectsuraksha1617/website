Karnataka Science and Technology Academy

Bharatesh Chakravarthi

Karnataka Science and Technology Academy (KSTA) had invited for technical papers throughout Karnataka to be a part of 9 th Annual KSTA conference on Science, Technology and Innovation for 21 st Century. Myself, Bharatesh Chakravarthi, Asst. Prof, Dept. of ISE had submitted one page abstract in the prescribed format of KSTA to clear the initial selection process. The submitted abstracted was selected and the same was conveyed to me over the mail. KSTA had given a format to prepare a poster to be presented in the conference.

The conference took place in Main Auditorium of Christ University, Hosur Road, Bnagalore on December 20 th and 21 st , 2016. The conference was inaugurated by honorable chief minister of Karnataka, Sri Siddaramaiah on 20 th at 10:00 am followed by the life time award presentation ceremony for Sri Dr. Kiran Kumar of ISRO.The delegates were asked to put up the posters in the exhibition hall and it was kept ready for the review process. The poster presented was all about the, Suraksha, The Digital Transformation to IPS project which is currently carried out as final year project.

The poster was reviewed by renowned person, Dr. Chnidananda Gowda and the results for announced on 22 nd at 11:00 am. It was my immense pleasure that, the poster was selected for second round which took place on the same day. Bangalore, walked through the opportunities and procedures for indo- korea exchange program, followed by the panel discussion and the valedictory.It was great experience to be a part of 9th annual KSTA conference and have gained lot of information which can be further shared among the students of our department. I sincerely thank Principal and HOD for permitting to participate in the conference.
