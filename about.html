About

FEEL SAFE WITH US!!!

    India being a democratic country, the Indian police system plays an important role as they provide security, which enhances civic trust and helps maintain social order.When common people become victims to negative events, its human tendency to panic.This app will help individuals to connect to the police,anytime and anywhere in India.

What are the modules that the app will have?

The app will consist of Criminal booking module, Case management module, Investigation module and Traffic monitoring module with accident rescue operations.

In what way the app different from the existing apps?

Using the app, the victims will be mapped to the nearest Police Station so that they can get help as soon as possible.
